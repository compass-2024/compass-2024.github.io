Welcome to COMPASS

Empowering future doctors through research, creativity, and collaboration.

Who We Are

Compass is a student-led club based at Shaheed Ziaur Rahman Medical College (SZMC), focused on medical education, research, and community outreach.

What We Do

We organize seminars, workshops, study circles, research training, and publish student-led journals and articles.

Join Us

Be a part of something impactful! Learn, teach, explore, and lead with Compass. Membership open to all passionate SZMC students.
